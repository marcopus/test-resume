What's this site

If you landed here, it's because you are either curious or looking for help.

I am a passionate problem solver and I can help you with your technical challenges.

In practice, I can help you with:

    Application design I can help you to design your application in a way that it is scalable, maintainable, and testable. I can help you to make the right technical decisions that will make your application future-proof.
    Refactoring Whether your project relies on old legacy code, it has evolved in a fast-paced environment, resulting in technical debt, chances are that it has become increasingly difficult to change. I can help you to simplify and modernize your application so that it becomes less painful for you and your team to evolve it.
    Microservices You've heard of them, you've read about them, you've seen them in action. But you are not sure how to start. I can help you to design and implement a microservices architecture that fits your needs.
    CI/CD An automated and robust process for building, testing, and deploying your application is a must-have in modern software development. I can help you to set up a CI/CD pipeline that fits your project and team.
    Agile teams I can guide your team in prioritizing fast iterations, shortening feedback loops, and delivering value to your users early on in the development cycle. I can help you to set up a DevOps culture within your team.

I have an agile mindset and I expect the same from our collaboration. I think in terms of business value when navigating the realm of technical decisions around complex software problems. That equates for me (and I hope also for you) maximizing users' satisfaction through safe, robust and useful software.

I don't build websites. Sorry.
